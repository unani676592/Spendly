Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

At Spendly, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Spendly personal expense tracking application (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1.Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly and a limited amount of technical data needed to run the Service:

  • Account information — such as your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you log.
  • Technical data — basic information like your browser type, needed to keep the Service secure and working.

2.How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the Service. This includes:

  • Displaying your expenses, summaries, and spending insights.
  • Maintaining and securing your account.
  • Responding to your support requests.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your financial information for advertising.

3.Data Storage

Your data is stored so that it is available to you whenever you sign in. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security, and you use the Service at your own discretion.

4.Third Party Services

We may rely on trusted third-party providers to help operate the Service, such as hosting and infrastructure providers. These providers only access your data to the extent necessary to perform their services and are expected to protect it.

Spendly is not responsible for the privacy practices of any external websites that may be linked from the Service.

5.Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We may update this policy from time to time, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
